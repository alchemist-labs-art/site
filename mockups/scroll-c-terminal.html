schema-aware ai

Manifold indexes your warehouse — tables, columns, types, relationships. When the AI writes a query, it's not guessing. It knows your data the way you do after six months on the job.

// you shouldn't need a "run" button.
// your editor should be as fast as your thinking.

reactive execution

Every change triggers an incremental re-computation. Results, charts, and data previews update as you type. The feedback loop collapses from minutes to milliseconds.

// lineage shouldn't live in a separate tool.
// it should be visible where you write your code.

visual lineage

See your entire pipeline as an interactive DAG, right in the editor. Trace column-level dependencies, understand impact before deploying, spot bottlenecks at a glance.
